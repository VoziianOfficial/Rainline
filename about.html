Company story

Built around the real way homeowners search for gutter help.

Gutter projects often begin with uncertainty: a leak near the roof edge, overflowing water during rain, aging sections pulling away from fascia, or a new exterior project that needs a complete gutter plan. Homeowners may not know whether to look for installation, replacement, repair, cleaning, or maintenance support.

Rainline was created to make that first step clearer. Instead of presenting itself as a local contractor, Rainline focuses on the matching process: helping users describe the request, choose the right service category, and compare independent provider options that may serve their area.

Aggregator model

Rainline helps organize the request, then homeowners compare providers.

Rainline is an independent matching platform. It is not a gutter contractor, does not employ gutter technicians, and does not perform gutter services directly. The purpose of the site is to help homeowners begin a request and connect with independent local providers.

Homeowners submit project details

Basic information such as service type, ZIP code, visible issue, timing, and contact details helps frame the request.

Provider options may vary by area

Availability can depend on location, project type, season, access conditions, and provider coverage.

Final hiring decisions are direct

Homeowners should compare quotes, credentials, terms, and scope directly with each independent provider.

Platform principles

Designed to feel simple, transparent, and practical.

Rainline keeps the experience focused on provider comparison, not direct contractor claims. The language, layout, and request flow are built to support clarity.

Clear role separation

The site clearly explains that providers are independent and Rainline does not guarantee or perform the work.

Comparison-focused flow

Homeowners are encouraged to compare quote scope, credentials, timing, materials, and provider terms.

Gutter-specific context

Requests are shaped around gutter installation, replacement, repair, cleaning, downspouts, drainage, and overflow concerns.

Easy contact path

The platform gives homeowners a simple way to start a request without searching through scattered listings.

Important clarity

Rainline is a matching platform, not the company doing the gutter work.

Any provider a homeowner may speak with through a matching flow is independent. Rainline does not control final pricing, appointment availability, project scope, materials, warranties, work quality, insurance status, or licensing. Those details should be confirmed directly before hiring.
