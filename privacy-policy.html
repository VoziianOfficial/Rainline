Policy details

Privacy information for homeowners using Rainline.

Rainline is not a gutter contractor and does not perform gutter installation, replacement, repair, cleaning, inspection, or maintenance directly. Rainline operates as an independent matching platform that may help homeowners connect with local service providers.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Rainline may collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit this website, submit a gutter-related request, call a listed number, interact with forms, or use related site features.

By using this website, you acknowledge that Rainline may process information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, you should not submit information through the website.

2. Information We May Collect

Rainline may collect information that you provide directly, including your name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, service category, project notes, timing preferences, and any other details you choose to submit through the website.

Rainline may also collect basic technical information, such as browser type, device information, general location signals, referring pages, pages viewed, interaction data, and other usage details that help maintain and improve the website.

3. How Information May Be Used

Rainline may use submitted information to process a matching request, communicate with you, help identify independent local provider options, improve website functionality, support quality and compliance efforts, respond to questions, and maintain records related to platform operations.

Information may also be used to understand service demand, improve form flows, prevent misuse, support security, and provide notices related to Rainline services, legal pages, or site policies.

4. Information Sharing

When you submit a gutter request, Rainline may share relevant information with independent local service providers, partner networks, vendors, or service platforms that may help respond to or process the request.

Rainline may also share information with vendors that support website hosting, analytics, communications, form handling, customer support, compliance, security, or other operational needs.

Rainline does not guarantee that any provider will contact you, provide a quote, accept a project, or offer service in your area. All providers are independent.

5. Your Choices

You may choose not to submit information through the website. You may also contact Rainline using the email listed on this website to ask questions about information you submitted.

If you receive communications after submitting a request, follow any available opt-out instructions or contact the sender directly when the communication comes from an independent provider.

6. Security

Rainline may use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect submitted information. However, no website, transmission method, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for use by adults seeking information about gutter provider matching. Rainline does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. Policy Updates

Rainline may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions may be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted means you acknowledge the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be sent to hello@rainlinematch.com .
